About Nyota Spaceworks

Mission: Nyota Spaceworks is committed to advancing Africa's presence in the space industry. We aim to pioneer innovative space technologies while providing opportunities for fellow Africans with a space dream.

Vision: To position Africa as a leader in the global space industry by fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable space exploration.
